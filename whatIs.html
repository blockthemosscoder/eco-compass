What is eco-friendly living?

Eco-friendly living is basicaly having conscious actions. It is about deciding harmless actions to the environment. You can be environmentally conscious by using renewable energy, less water and meat consumption, and reducing carbon footprint.

What are pollutions and environmental problem?

Pollutions are subtances that contaminate the environment and causes harm to living things. There are various types of pollutions, such as air pollution, land pollution and water pollution. And these pollutions causes environmental problem like global warming, endangered habitats and kill lives.
