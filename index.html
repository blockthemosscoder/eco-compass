Our Mission

We, The Council, seek to make an eco-friendly lifestyle that is sustainable and reliable. It is a lifestyle that will not effect the environment and utilize clean, reliable and eco-friendly energies and materials. To achieve this, we have made this website to bring attention to said eco-friendly lifestyles, and we hope that you can also adapt these into your own and perhaps do other things to help our cause.
