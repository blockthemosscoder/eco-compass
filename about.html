Our Story

Our path was initiated by the purpose of living with the care of the planet in mind. Disappointed and frustrated by waste and pollution, we resolved to make small changes that can collectively make a big difference. Therefore, by swapping single use plastics to reusable, or by choosing to use reusable products over non-reusable, we discovered that even if it's not a grand scale project, we can stil change the world by our collective acts. And thus, we created Eco-Compass, as a way to point the people of this planet towards eco friendly deeds, towards the end of most of our new world's troubles.

Our Mission

To reduce as much as environmental impacts. We figurd out humans are the main cause of polutions. The earth is getting more and more inhabitable because of us.We wanted to promote eco-friendly living and reduce our affects on the environment. We share tips, guides and tricks to reduce our impact. That is the reason we created this website called eco-compass.
