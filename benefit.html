Pros

Benefits of eco-friendly living can be divided into benefits we get and environmental benefits. First of all, eco-friendly living benefits us with reduced your energy usage, health benefits, reduced exposure to toxins, less maintainess costs, improved air quality and using eco-friendly landscapes for you properties can increase its value. Secondly, eco-friendly living is designed to reduce our environmental impacts and benefit it. So, here are benefits of eco-friendly living to the environment, eco-system and the wildlife.

  • Saves water and energy
  • Reduce carbon footprint and waste
  • protect biodiversity, eco-system and wildlife
  • Reduce pollutions

Cons

Nothing is perfect, so even something designed to do goods has flaws too.

  • Takes lots of time adapt and change
  • Has high materials costs
  • Need an alternative to specific items
  • Disruption of Traditional lifestyle
  • Need to adapt to new things and utilities

Even though it have these dissadvantages, it is still a very favored lifestyle. And the best ways to reduce pollutions and your environmental impacts.
