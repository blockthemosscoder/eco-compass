Will sustainable living ever become the global default?

Sustainable living may just become the global standard, but by necessity not choice. As the impacts of climate change fairly ramp up, and we begin to run short on resources globally, governments and industries will certainly be compelled to make eco-friendly choices. Those jurisdictions who are embracing change (from food districts to Nordic countries) show that this is possible, but widespread change will draw on many legislative changes, affordability of sustainable tech, and buying into the shift culturally.The move to sustainable living patterns is inevitable and the only question left is whether it wil happen fast enough, so that it doesn't cause irreparable harm to our planet.
